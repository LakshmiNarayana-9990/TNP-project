Our Vision

To become a quality department in Mechanical Engineering that makes its students well qualified, innovative contributors to their profession and society.

Our Mission

  • DM1: Educate and prepare the students to acquire good technical skills.
  • DM2: Provide better exposure of cutting edge technologies and strengthening Institute-Industry interaction to solve realistic problems.
  • DM3: To pursue higher studies inculcating lifelong learning capabilities and encouraging students to become an Entrepreneur.
  • DM4: To motivate quality research by both faculty and students addressing the core issues in Mechanical Engineering.
